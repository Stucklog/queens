Review in order and write only the decision-relevant note at each checkpoint. Start with the story pass, then the hero and cast, then chapter backgrounds and encounter sprites, and finish with palette and system consistency. Mark anything that needs a change with a clear action verb: rewrite, remove, add, reposition, recolor, rename, or verify.

### Fast review route

- [ ] Story pass — read the arc title, tagline, chapter titles, captions, and scene copy without looking at art.

- [ ] Consistency pass — compare palette, silhouette language, contrast, naming, and emotional escalation across chapters.
- [ ] Action pass — consolidate duplicate notes, rank fixes by impact, and record any final questions for implementation.

### Chapter 1 — A Crew for a Living Current

Turn four private motives into one workable plan.

**Scene beat:** Selke tests the crew against a model of the palace: Nima reshapes coral passages, Iri holds pressure doors on a note, and Jory crosses the mechanism without touching water. Their separate solutions work only after Selke charts them as one continuous route. Royal license collectors surround the hideout behind Brinehorn Bailiff, a salt-armored harbor guardian that tracks unpaid water through its branching crown. The crew combines all four specialties to send it charging after a decoy current, escaping with their plan and their first earned trust.

**Intent cue:** Escape with the plan

**Boss / escalation:** Brinehorn Bailiff

Steal the sequence that exists for one moonless hour.

Unfold a living current without letting the prison tear it apart.

### Chapter 8 — The Moving Ritual

Restore the high tide while leading an ancient danger home.

**Scene beat:** With the palace breaking around them, the crew distributes the seventh tide through reefs, ports, and inland channels in a sequence that becomes their escape route. Each restored waterway draws the awakened leviathan away from populated coasts and toward an ancient trench. Vask the Moonblind rises beneath the final current, following pressure older than any chart while the trench gates fail to align. Selke trusts her crew to finish the route without her command, and together they turn the restored tide into a moving ritual that guides Vask safely into the deep.

**Intent cue:** Guide the leviathan home
